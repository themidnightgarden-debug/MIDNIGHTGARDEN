About the Garden

The myth behind the magic.

Born between burnout and rebirth, The Midnight Garden is where Black feminine mysticism, digital design, and survival alchemy collide. This is art as ritual, not decoration.

The Midnight Garden x Murphy Made was built in the in-between: between paychecks and purpose, between gas station weed and herbal ritual, between “I’m exhausted” and “I’m not meant to live like this.”

Every print, planner, and ritual page here is designed to make your world feel more intentional, more enchanted, and more like the life you keep seeing in your head.
