Ritual Botanicals & Smoke-Free Altars

    Frame-worthy art that lists herbs, intentions and affirmations for each blend.
  • Apothecary Label Sets
    Printable labels for jars, tins and altar drawers in black, gold and bruised-orchid purple.
  • Ritual Cards
    Mini cards with blend names, correspondences and self-care prompts for spa-day altars.
