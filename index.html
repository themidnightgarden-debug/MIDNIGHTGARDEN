Midnight Botanica

The herbal heart of the brand: smoky florals, soft lungs and altar-side tea. Inspired by the blends you actually use: lavender, mullein, chamomile, peppermint, damiana and blue lotus.

Midnight Botanica is where ritual meets respiratory common sense. Each blend is designed for aesthetic and ritual use first – labels, artwork, recipe cards and print-on-demand posters – not as medical advice. Think “botanical mood board” you can frame, journal with or build gentle personal rituals around.

Signature Blend · Velvet Lungs

A balanced base featuring mullein and marshmallow leaf, lifted by lavender and chamomile, with a whisper of peppermint for clarity. Designed as a ritual concept blend for print, zines and recipe cards – a visual love letter to leaving gas-station nonsense behind.

Nothing on this site is a substitute for medical care, diagnosis or quitting aids. Herbs can interact with health conditions and medications – always do your own research and consult a professional before inhaling or ingesting anything new.

About the Studio

Midnight Garden by Murphy Made is the house where your spreadsheets, spell jars and soft-girl villain era all live together without arguing. It’s a print-on-demand studio built for women who are tired, clever and entirely done pretending they’re not powerful.

Every design starts as a ritual: a color story, a plant, a feeling. From there it becomes wall art, journals, textiles or digital tools – letting you build a life that feels like an altar, not an accident.

  • Parent company: Murphy Made – the umbrella for digital products, services and future brands.

  • Rooted in Black cats, roses, thorns, bones, silk, blood-red lips and old-world elegance.
